Your 6-step mantra (3+0)

Bf4 · Nc3 · Qd2 · f3 · O-O-O · g4. Develop the light-squared bishop, castle long by move 9–10, then storm with g4/h4. Only play a3 when Black pins with …Bb4. Do not chase …c5 with Nb5 early.

What went wrong — your loss vs sameertopno

Same Exchange start, but move 5 was 5.Nb5 chasing …c5 instead of 5.Bf4 and 6.Nc3. After …a6 you retreated to 6.Na3 — a knight on the rim with no follow-up. Black developed …Be6, …Nc6, and castled long while you were still untangling.

Position from your loss vs sameertopno · 2026-03-29 · verified via chess.js

The rule: Nc3 before tactics. Finish Bf4 · Qd2 · f3 · O-O-O, then g4 — the same rhythm that beat LordPablito. Save Nb5/Nxd6 tricks for when Black has already fallen behind in development.
