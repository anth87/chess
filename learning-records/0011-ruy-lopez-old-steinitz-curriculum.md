## What was covered

- **Old Steinitz Defence**: 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5 d6 (ECO C62)
- **Recurring loss pattern**: After White's standard setup (h3, O-O, c3, Re1, d4 exd4 cxd4), Black plays …a6 Ba4 b5 Bc2 h6 …Nh7. White plays Qd3 (passive); Black goes to g5, White trades, plays e5 which backfires, d4 falls.
- **Two identical losses confirmed**: vs kretenkonj (1749, resigned, 2026-06-19) and vs annapurna317 (1870, checkmated, 2026-02-27) — same move sequence, same collapse.
- **Correct response**: 13.d5 immediately — closes the centre, removes g5's tactical point, Nc6 must retreat. Follow with Nd2 then f4.
- **Wrong response**: 13.Qd3 — looks like it defends d4 but doesn't stop …Ng5. After Nxg5 Bxg5 and failed e5 push, d4 falls to …Nxd4.
