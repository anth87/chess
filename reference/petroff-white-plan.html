Petroff Classical — White vs 3…d6 4…Nxe4

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · Classical Variation (C42)

1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nf6 3.Nxe5 d6 4.Nf3 Nxe4 5.Nc3 Nxc3 6.dxc3
  1. Bf4 — develop the bishop before choosing a castle side
  2. Qd2 — connect rooks, set up long castle (before any kingside moves)
  3. O-O-O — castle long; king to c1, rook to d1 on the open file
  4. h4 · g4 — begin the kingside pawn storm once the king is safe
  5. h5 / g5 — push pawns to prise open the kingside against Black's king
Avoid: Bc4 then O-O — short castle lets Black equalise with …d5 or …Nc6, and you earn nothing from the position.
