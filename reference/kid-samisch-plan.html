KID Samisch — Black vs g4/h5

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · Samisch (3.f3) kingside attack

1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 g6 3.f3 Bg7 4.e4 d6 5.Be3 O-O 6.Qd2 Nbd7
  1. …e5 — when White plays g4, strike the centre (not the pawn on g4)
  2. …Nc5 — occupy the outpost after …e5 and d5
  3. …h6 — when White plays h5, blunt the attack (do not play …Nxg4)
  4. …b5 — queenside counterplay only after the centre is stable
Avoid: …Nxg4 — it opens the f-file, activates White's pieces, and you lose the initiative you fought for with …e5.
