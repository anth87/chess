CCT — Before You Move

Checks · Captures · Threats · 3+0 habit card

When: After your opponent moves, before you touch a piece — especially when material is loose, the king is exposed, or you are low on time.
  1. Checks — Can they give check next move? If I play my move, does a check fork or force something awful?
  2. Captures — What can they take for free or in a sequence? What can I take — and is the recapture safe?
  3. Threats — What are they threatening besides the obvious capture? (mate threats, forks, pins, pawn pushes)

Only after CCT: pick your candidate move, then run CCT again for your own move ("what if I play this?").
