anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · 15 losses in 2.Bc4 branch (12 mo)

1.e4 d6 2.Bc4 Nf6 3.d3 g6 4.Nf3 Bg7 5.Bg5
  1. g6 · Bg7 — fianchetto before any centre pawns
  2. O-O — castle on move 5 vs Bg5; king safety beats …c6
  3. …c5 later — hit d4 once development is done (Lessons 2, 8, 10)
  4. vs 3.Bxf7+ Kxf7 — …Nc6 · …Bg7 fast; never …h6/…g5 chasing Bg5
Avoid: …c6 before castling — blocks your Bg7 and leaves the king in the centre. …h6/…g5 chasing Bg5 hands White permanent kingside targets.
