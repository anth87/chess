Philidor Exchange — White vs 1…e5 2…d6

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · Exchange …exd4 4.Nxd4

1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 d6 3.d4 exd4 4.Nxd4
  1. Bf4 (or Be3) — stake out the centre before chasing tactics
  2. Nc3 — develop; do not play Nb5 knight dances early
  3. Qd2 — connect rooks, support long castle
  4. f3 — anchor g4; stops …Ng4 tricks
  5. O-O-O — castle long by move 9–10; king safe on c1
  6. g4 — kingside pawn storm; only then open lines with h4/f4
Avoid: 5.Nb5 or 9.Ndb5 before finishing development — you lose time and Black catches up with …c5/…a6.
