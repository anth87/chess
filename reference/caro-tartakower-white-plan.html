Caro-Kann Tartakower — White vs 6…Bd6

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · Forgacs / Tartakower …exf6 structure

1.e4 c6 2.d4 d5 3.Nc3 dxe4 4.Nxe4 Nf6 5.Nxf6+ exf6 6.Bc4 Bd6
  1. Be3 — develop the dark-squared bishop before opening lines
  2. Qd2 — connect rooks, support long castle (before Nf3)
  3. O-O-O — castle long by move 9; king safe on c1
  4. Bd3 — trade when …Bf5 pins; recapture with the queen
  5. Nf3 — develop the knight after the king is castled
  6. g4 — kingside pawn storm against the shattered f-pawns
Avoid: 7.Nf3 before Qd2/O-O-O — …Bg4 pins and Bxf3 wrecks your kingside pawns.
