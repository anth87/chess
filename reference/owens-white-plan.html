Owen's Defense — White vs 1…b6

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · offbeat 1…b6 / Owen's

1.e4 b6 2.d4 Bb7 3.Nc3 e6
  1. Be3 — develop toward the kingside attack (not a3 unless …Bb4 pins)
  2. Qd2 — connect rooks, support long castle on the next move
  3. O-O-O — castle long by move 6–7; king safe, rook eyes the d-file
  4. Bb5 — pin/grab tempo on …Nc6; then f4/g4 or Bc4 and a pawn storm
Avoid: 4.a3 when Black has not played …Bb4 — you lose a tempo and let Black finish …Be7/…d6 comfortably.
