Pirc Classical — Black vs 5.Bc4

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · Your most-played loss line

1.e4 d6 2.Nf3 Nf6 3.Nc3 g6 4.d4 Bg7 5.Bc4 O-O 6.O-O Nbd7
  1. Fianchetto + castle — …g6, …Bg7, …O-O before touching the center
  2. …Nbd7 — connects rooks, supports …e5 or …c5 later
  3. When White plays e5: …dxe5 dxe5 — open the d-file, expose the e-pawn
  4. …Ng4 — attack the e5 pawn; knight belongs here in this line
  5. …c5 next — strike d4; your counterplay, not passive trades
Avoid: …Bxc3 and queen trades when you have not played …c5 — you hand White the bishop pair and a comfortable queenless middlegame.
