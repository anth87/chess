Berlin / Zukertort — White vs 3…Nf6 4…Bc5

anth87 leak-fix · 3+0 blitz · Ruy Lopez sidelines (not Berlin endgame)

1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5 Nf6 4.O-O Bc5
  1. c3 — support d4; deny …Bb4 pins without preparation
  2. Re1 — pressure the e-pawn; connect rooks after d4
  3. h3 — stop …Bg4/…Ng4 tricks before opening the centre
  4. d4 — strike the centre once the kingside is safe
  5. Nc3 — develop before Bg5; …Nxd4 forks if the bishop is on h4
Avoid: 10.Bg5/11.Bh4 before Nc3 when …Nxd4 is in the air — the knight fork wins material.
